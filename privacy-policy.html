Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Days Calculator is an Android application designed to help users calculate the number of remaining office and remote work days required to meet quarterly work balance requirements.

The application was created primarily for employees of Getinge whose employment contracts require them to complete approximately 40% of their working days from the office and 60% remotely within each quarter.

Data Collection

Days Calculator does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All calculations are performed locally on the user's device. The application does not send any information to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The application does not use analytics, advertising networks, or third-party services.

Internet Access

The application does not require internet access for its functionality.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with the developer or any third parties.
